Project Wolverine

It is a period of fun times. Countless trivia questions for you and your friends. Once in the Trivia room, read the question at the top of the screen. You will have little time to choose the correct answer.

If you answer your question correctly you will receive points. The amount of points you get depends on how quickly you answer. So answer quickly to maximize your points.

If you answer with time to spare, you will be directed to the waiting room where you can see your response compared to the other players.
